DJ GlitchWitch.fm

Known for throwing unforgettable raves and electrifying live music events, GlitchWitch.fm has recently stepped behind the decks as a DJ. Learning directly from legendary humans such as LSDave, Pigeon, Nibs, Upcycle Sounds, and Ali Khan, they played their debut set at December 2025's Apothecary Afters at SkullSpace. Always working to bring their infectious energy and a meticulously curated setlist to the dance floor, Jade is available to DJ events — book via Instagram or email!

The Attic

In summer 2025, Jade founded "The Attic": a DIY house venue devoted to queer and anti-capitalist values, hosting live shows and nurturing community connection in Winnipeg. Local and traveling artists alike have found a creative haven here.

SkullSpace

Since spring 2025, Jade has immersed themselves in Winnipeg's local hackerspace, SkullSpace, leading major initiatives and fostering a welcoming environment for both newcomers and veteran makers. Their impacts can be felt across the space, from the improved DJ booth, to the complete reorganization of the space, and the revamped energy and structure around hosting fun, safe, and creative events that bring the community together while helping the space thrive.
